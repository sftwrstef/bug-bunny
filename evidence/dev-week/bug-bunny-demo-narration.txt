Bug Bunny is an evidence console for authorized Web research.

The scope gate records the target and explicit approval. One click runs a bounded, read-only Web audit to capture headers, routes, cookies, CORS behavior, and reproduction commands.

Surface Map shows the safely reached routes and candidate signals.

When a claim needs proof, the same-state IDOR replay proves legitimate access, then demonstrates victim-private data exposure.

The final report packages commands, evidence, and status. Built in Codex with GPT-5.6 Sol, Bug Bunny converts real signals into verifiable reports.
